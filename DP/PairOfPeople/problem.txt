Number of ways to pair people


Given that there are p people in a party. 
Each person can either join dance as a single 
individual or as a pair with any other. 
The task is to find the number of different 
ways in which p people can join the dance.



Input : p = 3
Output : 4
Explanation: Let the three people be P1, P2 and P3
Different ways are: {P1, P2, P3}, {{P1, P2}, P3}, {{P1, P3}, P2} and {{P2, P3}, P1}.
